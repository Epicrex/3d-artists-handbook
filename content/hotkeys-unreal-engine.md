## Navigation/ Transformation
|Function|Key|Notes
|:-:|:-:|:-:
|Navigate|**`WASD`** + **`MB`**
|Stick to object|**`Shift`**
|Focus Selected|**`F`**
|Move / Rotate / Scale mode|**`W / E / R`**
